AS SEEN IN

Creating healthy peanut butter one day at a time

Uncle Sam's Corndogs is a family-run business bringing the authentic taste of American corndogs to London. The company was founded by David, who lived in Los Angeles for 30 years, and his son Dylan, a chef at Soho House in London. With David's extensive market experience and Dylan's culinary expertise, they have crafted corndogs that combine high-quality ingredients with a perfect golden crunch. Trading at Chatsworth Market every Sunday and recently debuting at Broadway Market, Uncle Sam's Corndogs aims to fill a gap in London's street food scene by offering a beloved American classic. Each corndog is made with care, ensuring a delightful blend of cheesy, crispy goodness in every bite. Discover why these corndogs are becoming a must-try treat for Londoners.
